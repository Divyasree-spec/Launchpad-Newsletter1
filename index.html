IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024

Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024, a collaborative effort between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, stands as a premier job fair aimed at fostering opportunities for engineering students and graduates across Kerala. This distinguished event connects talented individuals with innovative companies in the technical and engineering domains, creating a platform for meaningful career advancements and industry collaborations.

Call to Action

Event Roadmap

  1. Students and graduates register to showcase their skills and expertise.
  2. Participants engage in a rigorous technical challenge designed to assess their problem-solving abilities and technical prowess.
  3. Qualified participants proceed to an online test evaluating both technical and non-technical skills, ensuring comprehensive evaluation for further stages.
  4. Shortlisted candidates have the opportunity to choose interview locations in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, culminating in face-to-face interviews with prospective employers.
  5. Compilation of rankings based on performance in assessments, guiding companies in selecting top-tier candidates.
  6. Selected candidates receive notifications and choose interview locations based on their preferences.
  7. Notification to selected candidates for interviews.
  8. Final round where candidates meet prospective employers for career opportunities.
